In the spring of 2017 we completed a large foldout brochure map of Vernon County, WI, commissioned by [Vernon Trails](http://vernontrails.com/). The map shows detailed information about 12 bike routes, including road surface information and elevation profiles. The map is distributed free around Viroqua.
