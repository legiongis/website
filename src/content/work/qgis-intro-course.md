In the spring and summer of 2016, we partnered with the [Valley Stewardship Network](https://valleystewardshipnetwork.org/) in Viroqua, WI, to develop and teach an Introduction to QGIS course. The course was offered to the community two separate times, and consisted of four weekly two-hour sessions.

The materials are [available on GitHub](https://github.com/mradamcox/qgis-intro), so anyone can use or contribute to them.
